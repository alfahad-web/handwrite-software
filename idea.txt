letter, we will have checkboxes for l & r. checking l will mean the letter is written to
be left joinable. same for r.
then when we put two letters, we pick randomly, and when two adjacent letters are right 
joinable and left joinable, then we interpolate their points to join them and it generates
a joined text. we skip this check when we have a space.
